you can use this material in the board game:
- less than 4 dice
- A3 paper for board
- piece less than 16
- one sets of poker cards (54 cards includes four suits (hearts, diamonds, clubs, spades) and each suit has 13 cards (Ace through King) and two jokers)

the constraint of the board game is:
- 4 people game
- playing in a room 5m square
- played in about 30 minutes
- all components must be prepaired within the material.
